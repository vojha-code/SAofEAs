Algorithms

The list of algorithms available in YEPA follows in alphabetic order. You may use any of these algorithms to solve a defined optimization problem, interchangeably. However, some parameter tunning must be applied to get the best result possible on a specific optimization problem.

  • Artificial Bee Colony (ABC)
  • Continuous Ant Colony Optimization (ACOR)
  • Bees Algorithm (BA)
  • Biogeography-based Optimization (BBO)
  • Covariance Matrix Adaptation Evolution Strategy (CMA-ES)
  • Differential Evolution (DE)
  • Firefly Algorithm (FA)
  • Genetic Algorithm (GA)
  • Harmony Search (HS)
  • Imperialist Competitive Algorithm (ICA)
  • Invasive Weed Optimization (IWO)
  • Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)
  • Simulated Annealing (SA)
  • Teaching-Learning-based Optimization (TLBO)
